About us
Wandering in flowers is a brand focusing on the development of flower tea industry. The meaning of the name is our expectation for flower tea. We hope that when you drink the flower tea, there will be clusters of flowers blooming in your mouth, as if you are swimming in the sea of flowers.

The brand originated from the interest of our team. We believed that the development of the flower tea series was an extension of our shared interests, and therefore Wandering in flowers was born. The current solar terms series are represented by the solar terms with climate change in spring, summer, autumn and winter, and the common seasonal troubles are used as the starting point to develop the corresponding series of flower tea.
Product introduction
Spring Flora

The climate is often unstable and the temperature differs a lot between day and night in spring. Our main product of the spring is the sweet flower nectar that can adjust our mood. Dry rose petals, strawberries, peaches and other dried fruits are placed in the tea bag to create a beautiful day. The sweetness and romance in the mouth can maintain a happy mood even in the unpredictable weather.
Summer Rain

Transpiration of summer heat is often caused by the lack of appetite due to dry heat and edema because of high temperature and humidity. Therefore, our summer tea is especially based on refreshing ingredients such as roselle, lemon, black beans, and Job's tears to make a cool and appetizing herbal tea. These ingredients are not only appetizing but can also eliminate edema and improve endocrine disorders. Drink a cup of our tea when suffering from the heat. You can feel the bursts of fresh charm inside of your mouth.
Drunken Autumn Breeze

The Beginning of Autumn and Cold Dew represent the gradual coolness of autumn, and the weather leads to a torridity and unstable skin condition. It is appropriate to have nourishing ingredients that have the effect of cooling and ameliorating our body. In this season, it is suitable to drink a cup of our tea containing chrysanthemum, honeysuckle, hawthorn, and cassia seeds, which helps to stabilize our body in autumn.
Plum and Snow

Great Snow and Great Cold represent the cold in winter, and it is easy to have poor blood circulation, dry skin, and even a cold. At this time, it is suitable to drink a cup of our scented tea containing Chinese medicinal materials to moisturize and drive the cold away from your body. The ingredients contain wolfberry, red dates, ginger slices, and rose flowers, which can not only improve your body but also feel the warmth in the tea.
