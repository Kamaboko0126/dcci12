ABOUT WHO AM I
Have you lost your way on the stagnant road?
In this society where you needed labor to exchange for money, when have your enthusiasm started to die out?

Working hard for the company all day long, but forgetting about your value, sometimes you might ask yourself, have you become what you want for your dream? Or has it been worn away by reality?

" Who am I '' uses creations to narrate the blood, tears, and social struggles of the wage slaves, weave the dream of resistance that the wage slaves dare not think of, create spiritual victory, and break the chains with black comedy!
