Misstory
Have you ever thought about escaping from your busy life?
Have you ever felt lonely in this busy world?

Misstory is an immersive mobile game.
The story begins with a dog living in our leading role’s memory.
As time passes by, this memory is blurred.
However, she could never forgot the secret deeply buried in her mind for decades.

With the arrival of other pets,
the leading role found out that every pet has similar features with the dog dwelling in her memory,
and the dusty memory is about to flash out.

A heart-warming pet hotel located in the alley of a busy city.
“Are you ready to open the gate of Misstory”?
